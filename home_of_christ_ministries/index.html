Vision

Reaching the unreached and the wrongly reached with the true Gospel (Good News), making members into leaders and leaders of leaders by the Gospel.
— 2 Corinthians 5:16-21

Mission Statement

  • The goodness of Jesus Christ who delivered us out of the domain of darkness into the eternal kingdom.
  • Right relationships and a gentle defense of the Gospel.
  • Changing lives and reconciling them to Jesus Christ.
  • Grounding believers through teaching and discipleship.
  • Initiating projects to help those in need.
  • Making the impossible possible through prayer and worship.
  • Encouraging Bible reading, study, and prayerful lifestyle.

Statement of Faith

  • We believe in one True God in three persons:
  • God the Father
  • God the Son
  • God the Holy Spirit
